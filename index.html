My name is John Moorman, a front-end developer and automation engineer based in Berlin, Germany. I have worked with clients in the arts, technology, and charity sectors to build pixel-perfect websites using their preferred technologies, from plain HTML/CSS/JS, to Vue+TypeScript+Tailwind, React, Wordpress, and more.

Skilled in problem-solving, customer service, and team collaboration, I also worked with these clients to analyze their workflow and build bespoke automations to save time and enhance their businesses (technologies include: Google Apps Script and Workspace, Microsoft Office, Make.com, CCB, and more).

My Work

A small sample of the professional work I've done.

Rob Moorman Music

Rob Moorman Music needed a site to display their work, sell their merch, and provide updates on their events. They wanted to have the ability to make changes and updates themselves, so I designed and set their site up with Wordpress and taught them the basics of its use.

finalflow

I worked with finalflow to create their website as an SPA, using their preferred technologies of Vue with Tailwind. The site was created precisely according to their Figma designs and features the ability to sign up for a waitlist and create an account, authenticated via Firebase.

Berlin Opera Academy

I built this client's website from scratch with frequent communication and adjustments to their needs using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

Business Automation

For a previous client, I automated their largely spreadsheet-based workflow with extensive use of Google Apps Script. I wrote custom formulae, connected their spreadsheets to various external APIs (including Slack and Twilio), generated emails and reminders, organized their data into a searchable database, and much more.

All of this code is proprietary, but I am happy to discuss specifics and processes with those who are interested.
