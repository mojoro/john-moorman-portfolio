## The Problem

A wellness retreat needed their web presence modernised. The existing frontend was outdated, analytics were nonexistent, data pipelines were fragile, and their booking calendar had no integration with their internal systems.

## What I Built

This was a multi-faceted engagement covering frontend, backend, and infrastructure:

- **Frontend rebuild** — Modernised the client-facing site with improved responsiveness and performance
- **Analytics implementation** — Set up Google Analytics with custom event tracking to give the client visibility into visitor behaviour and booking funnel metrics
- **Data pipeline migration** — Moved fragile manual data flows to more reliable automated processes
- **Custom PHP calendar plugin** — Built a bespoke plugin to sync their booking calendar with internal management systems via REST APIs

## Constraints

Client confidentiality limits what I can share about the specifics. The technical depth here was less about any single system and more about managing multiple workstreams for a non-technical client — translating business needs into technical deliverables and shipping incrementally.
